Corey Ford

PhD Researcher in AI, HCI and Music at Queen Mary University of London

HCI researcher with specialisms in creativity support tools, human-AI interaction and audio. My research broadly explores the role of technologies (such as Generative AI) in creative user experiences (such as music composition).

Areas: Creativity Support Tools, Designing for Reflection, Music Technology, User Experience Design, Interaction Design, Computer-Supported Education, Music Composition, Generative Music, Explainable AI, Machine Learning, NIME, P5js & Processing, C++
